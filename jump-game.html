How to Play

Controls
Hold anywhere on the screen to charge power, then release to jump. The longer you hold, the farther you go.

Scoring
Each landing scores 1 point. Landing on the center earns bonus points — consecutive center hits multiply your bonus.

Combo
Hit the center of consecutive platforms to build your combo (×2, ×3, ×4...). Missing the center resets it to zero.

Platforms
Platforms come in various shapes and sizes. Smaller ones appear more as difficulty increases. Landing on edges is risky — you may slip off!

Tips
Watch the power bar to gauge distance. Aim for the center dot — consistent hits rack up massive combo bonuses.
